About us

Hash Academy is an Africa-based Blockchain Talent Development and Outsourcing company. We provide organizations from all around the globe the skills and talents of the most competent and experienced Blockchain Engineers of the African continent. All our engineers are fully vetted to ensure that they deliver the best value for money and have the technical and communication skills to become integral and core contributors to the development of your Blockchain projects.

In addition to our Team Augmentation services, Hash Academy also provides Blockchain capacity development to engineers interested in building innovation applications for the Web 3.0, thus increasing the global talent pool of much-needed blockchain engineers.

Our vision is to create a world where talented African technology professionals can move where they are needed to help businesses innovate and grow.

Our success is rooted in the success of our customers, so we actively contribute to the growth of their companies.

As a reliable technology partner, we strive to help our customers make their business smarter, adopt new technologies and be more competitive in the marketplace.

Customers choose us for our timely and effective communication, our willingness to build long-lasting and trusting relationships, our technical expertise, our strong desire and our ability to face all possible challenges as well as our flexibility.
